Lying

That's almost a natural sin, pretty much every human has lied in their life before which even the bible confirms this in Romans 3:4-8

Proverbs 19:9 tells us that even people who constantly lie will find death

proverbs 12:22 tells us that God can't use liars and gives important tasks to the trustworthy

LIAR

Stealing

1 Corinthians 6:10 Tells us that Thieves wont inherit the kingdom of God

Ephesians 4:28 tells us that the ones who steal should stop stealing and start sharing instead

Proverbs 6:30 tells us to not hate people who steal to survive, if he steals food or some blanket, God does not condone stealing to survive but it also tells us that if hes caught during the act, he will have to repay 7 times as much

But always remember, be generous to the poor and the needy just like Jesus was and still is.

THIEF

Blaspheming

Words like "oh my god" that people use is called blasphemy which is even in the 10 commandments (Exodus 20:7)

Leviticus 19:12 tells us that God does not want his name to be used in vain, He is the almighty and deserves respect.

Philippians 2:9 tells us that the name Jesus holds power thus using his name in vain, he will help less

BLASPHEMER

so is someone who insults God

Hatred

1 John 4:20 tells us that someone that hates others, cannot love God.

Proverbs 10:12 is about how hatred creates conflicts and love covers up for errors

1 John 2:9 tells us that whoever thinks they're good, but hates someone, isnt good

1 John 3:15 tells us that whoever hates is a MURDERER BY HEART

Lusting

Its probably one of the most common sin since there's so much pornography and other sexual content on the internet tempting people to commit this sin

Matthew 5:27-30 tells us that lusting at a woman is committing adultery in the heart and God hates this sin

1 John 2:16 tells us that God did not give us lust, but the world did, and pride is connected to it

2 timothy 1:7 tells us that God gave us a spirit of power, love and self control.

ADULTERER BY HEART

Fornication and Adultery

Throwing both in the same pot, they are both related to another:
Fornication means having sexual intercourse with someone you're not married with
Adultery means having sexual intercourse or even a relationship with someone who is not your wife/husband even tho you're married, basically Cheating

1 Corinthians 6:18 tells us that fornicating and cheating/adultery is against the body not just God

1 Corinthians 7:2 tells us that every man should only have 1 wife and every woman only 1 husband concerning as much the adulterers and the fornicators

Revelation 21:8 tells us that sexual immoral people will go to hell

FORNICATOR / ADULTERER

Idolatry

People that take certain things from the bible and consider parts of the bible fake/false are also creating their own god/gods and are also idolaters

1 Corinthians 10:14 tells us to literally run from idolatry

Exodus 20:4 is in the 10 commandments and tells us not to make Idols

Colossians 3:5 tells us to crucify our flesh that wants to commit idolatry with earthly things.

IDOLATER

Sorcery/Witchcraft/Magic

Magic isn't power or anything from the spirit, its demonic, demons help you perform magic in real life by misleading you

Revelation 21:8 tells us that sorcerers will burn in hell.

In 2 Chronicles, Manasseh has been sacrificing children and has made himself more and more guilty before the lord

Firstly, God does not want human sacrifice, less even child sacrifice, and secondly, you shall not worship idols

Galatians 5:16-26 tells us that sorcerers wont inherit the kingdom of God

WITCH/SORCERER

Homosexuality/Sodomy

Todays world connected homosexuality sadly with pride.

Nowadays homosexuality has misused and put bad stains on a creation God has made for us, the rainbow stands for the part God has promised us that he will never flood the world again as in genesis 9:1-17

1 Corinthians 6:9-11 tells us that homosexuals will not enter the kingdom of God

1 Timothy 1:8-11 tells us that homosexuality goes against the sound doctrine

romans 1:26-27 tells us that homosexuality is something shameful and awaits penalty

HOMOSEXUAL/SODOMITE

Drunkenness

Many people think that alcohol is the solution to depression, but its not true, God gave us a spirit of control as in 2 timothy 1:7

1 Corinthians 6:9-10 and Galatians 5:21 tells us that drunkards wont inherit the kingdom of God

1 Corinthians 5:11 and Proverbs 23:20-21 tells us to stay away from immoral people including the drunkards

Ephesians 5:18-20 tells us to not get drunk instead seek and worship God.

DRUNKARD

Murder

Revelation 21:8 tells us that murderers go burn in hell

Matthew 15:19 tells us that murder is something that comes from the heart to begin with, be it hatred or dark desires

In Matthew 19.16-22 Jesus said that murderers won't get eternal life

MURDERER

Slander

Slander is making fun of people and saying bad things about them

Psalm 101:5 tells us that God destroys whoever slanders others

Matthew 12:36 says that every word we say in life will be judged by God

Proverbs 8:13 tells us to stop using hate speech against humans

Note: hate speech is specifically things like racism or badmouthing someone, coming with arguments against someones ideology is not hate speech.

SLANDERER

Pride

Pride is the source to many many sins, its also what made satan fall in the first place

proverbs 8:12-21 explains how God doesn't like pride and arrogance.

Philippians 2:3 tells us that God wants us to come in humility and not in pride

Proverbs 16:5 tells us that the proud are an abomination to God

HAUGHTY

Unforgivable Sin 🔗

Denying God/Blasphemy Against the Holy Spirit

The unforgivable sin isn't a quick and accidental sin, its a LONG AND WILLFUL one

Matthew 12:31, Mark 3:28 and Luke 12:10 say that blasphemy against the Holy Spirit cannot be forgiven.

Knowing the truth but denying it leads one day to the unforgivable sin, pharaoh from the book of exodus committed the unforgivable sin by hardening his heart continuously until God accepted his choice and left him in the darkness

God seeks a relationship with you, and constantly tries getting your attention, willfully denying him will make him no more seek you, until the point you cannot even find him anymore

There's an invisible line you cross one day by denying Jesus in which one day you cannot accept Jesus anymore and become lost

External Factors 🔗

1 corinthians 10:13 tells us that God does not allow temptation beyond of what you can bear, he will protect you against it, if it goes too far, but God does not tempt people as in James 1:13-15

Satan is always tempting people, he cannot do anything else but tempting, Satan is already a defeated enemy.

THE ONLY HOPE 🔗

Everyone is a sinner, and no one deserves heaven, but Jesus gave his Life for US all.
So that we can be forgiven and through his GRACE only, will we get eternal LIFE.

John 5:24, John 14:6, John 1:29

Ephesians 2:8 tells us that faith in Jesus saves us, not anything else

We can't pay our way into heaven, the death of Jesus is sufficient as in 1 John 2:2

Through the blood of Jesus alone are we saved

Why believe in Jesus?

1 Corinthians 15:12-32 tells us that if Jesus did not die on the cross, we couldn't have our sins forgiven
